Gant diagram - Innovation project - laser cutter

Social impact

Its applications will be related to the school, office, architects, carpenters and students. Yes, because it will solve the problem to several teachers and clerks at the level of the whole Arequipa, since its usefulness will be very necessary, and will have a impact before the buyers of this machine, since the need of professors, architects, etc, will be necessary, because they will be able to cut small pieces for their work, projects, scale models, adornments in an exact and precise way.
